Airbnb Perfect Price, charge the ideal price for your Airbnb accomodation with ease

Welcome to Airbnb Perfect Price! This web app is designed to help you optimize your Airbnb pricing strategy. Here's how the app works:

  • The predictive model (random forest) calculates the ideal price for your Airbnb listing based on the most relevant features of your accommodation.
  • The app offers two versions: one for new Airbnb host and one for users that already own an Airbnb accommodation.
  • Accurate and reliable market price estimations are provided by the app, so you can maximize your Airbnb revenue easily.

Current Limitations and Future Plans

Note that our app has some limitations and there is areas for improvement. Here's what you need to know:

  • The price calculated by our app is fixed for the whole year and does not take into account seasonal trends. However, this can be an advantage as it simplifies the pricing process for hosts and ensures that customers know what to expect when booking your listing.
  • The app is updated frequently, but users may need to recalculate their listing price from time to time. This is particularly important to to follow inflation rates change.
  • I am working to improve the accuracy of the predictions. New input features may be included in the next future.
