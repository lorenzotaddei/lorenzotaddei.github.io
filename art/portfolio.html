Bringing your vision to blackboard

So, you've got a vision for a stunning blackboard that'll give the right touch for your bar or restaurant. Well, you're in the right place! Here's how our creative collaboration unfolds:

  • Get in touch: You take the first step and reach out. Share your vision (whether you have an idea in mind or not, we can brainstorm). Let me know the size of your blackboard (whether you have one already or need a new one), the content, and your style and color preferences.
  • From idea to sketch : Once I have your input, I'll work my things and sketch out your dream blackboard. If it well fits with your taste, we're well on our way!
  • Pricing that Fits: Now, let's talk numbers, little numbers. The cost is determined mostly by the materials I will need, and also time required. You'll find it's the affordablest.
  • The final step: I bring your vision to life on your blackboard, turning it into a captivating work of art.

And the best part? Up until the sketch, it's absolutely free!
