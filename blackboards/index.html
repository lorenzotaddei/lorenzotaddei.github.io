Bringing your vision to blackboard

So, you've got a vision for a stunning blackboard that'll give the right touch to your bar or restaurant. Well, you're in the right place! Here's how our creative collaboration unfolds:

  • Get in touch: You take the first step and reach out. Share your vision, whether it is a specific one or not. Let me know the size of the blackboard, the content, and your style and color preferences.
  • From idea to sketch: Once I have your input, I'll do my things and sketch out your dream blackboard. If it well fits with your taste, we're good to move on! Otherwise we'll go for a second round.
  • Pricing that Fits: Once the sketch is done we can talk numbers, small numbers. The cost is determined mostly by the materials I will need. You'll find it very affordable.
  • The final step: I bring your vision to life on your blackboard, turning it into a captivating work of art.

And the best part? Up until the sketch, it's absolutely free!
